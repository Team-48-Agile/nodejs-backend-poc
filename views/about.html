What is Bionic Reading?

Bionic Reading is a new reading method that works by creating fixation points in a text that help guide the reader's eyes, helping readers to read long passages of text quickly and effortlessly.

Why not try it yourself? Compare your experience reading the two texts below.

The Benefits of Bionic Reading

Bionic Reading has so much more to offer than just increasing reading speed.

We believe that Bionic Reading can not only help you to read faster, but boost concentration and engagement. Bionic Reading has the potential to transform the reading experience for all!

In particular, Bionic Reading can help:

  • Those with accesibility concerns or visual impairments
  • Those with ADHD
  • Those thast struggle to read long passages online
